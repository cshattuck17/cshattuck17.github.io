About

Courtney Shattuck is a highly motivated Producer/Director/Shooter/Editor/On-Air Talent combination package. She has been employed at WEEK-TV/WHOI-19 for 3.5 years, and in the position of "Good Company" Producer for 2 years. "Good Company" is a sales-based, lifestyle talk-show that is on weekdays at 5pm on the ABC affiliate, WHOI-19, in Peoria, IL. Courtney works closely with Account Executives to ensure clients segments are exceptionally produced and exceed expectations. When she took over the producer position in 2013, sales were at $9k monthly. Now, 2 years later, the show is generating a monthly revenue of $16k +! Courtney is proficient in EDIUS, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Photoshop, ENPS, Avid and Final Cut Pro. She also fills in as a Co-Host, and hosts 2 monthly on-location segments. Anytime a Director is off sick or on vacation, Courtney fills in as the Director for WHOI-19 News "Daybreak," a 2-hour morning newscast. She also live directs and technical directs at Northwoods Community Church on the weekends. She is trained on both a Ross Overdrive Automation switcher, as well as directing a full crew using a Ross Vision switcher. From client relationships to shooting in the field, hosting segments to producing, Courtney wears many hats and loves every role she has to fill!
